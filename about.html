Why Are We Here

KickHERassMENt.com's purpose is to provide a source of education on what harassment in the workplace looks like and the impacts it has on people's lives. If you are curious as to why your company may be lacking diversity or are interested in learning better ways to identify what harassment may look like in your workplace, kickHERassMENt provides real-life stories from victims of harassment, a simulation that allows you to see exactly what types of harassment occur in the workplace, and a Q&A section where you can gain advice from people who have experienced situations involving harassment. It is very important to kickHERassMENt to emphasize the importance of all types of harassment regardless of gender, race, religion, or sexual orientation.
